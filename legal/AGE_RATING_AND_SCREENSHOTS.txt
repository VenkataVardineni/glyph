App Store Connect — Age rating & screenshots (Glyph)
======================================================

AGE RATING
----------
Random / stranger live video with UGC typically maps to a higher age band (often 17+ in the US questionnaire), especially if content is user-generated and not fully pre-moderated before display. Answer the ASC questionnaire honestly based on your shipped safety model. Legal counsel recommended.

SCREENSHOTS (Guideline 2.3)
----------------------------
• Use captures from real builds (simulator or device). Do not use misleading pre-rendered marketing art that does not match the UI.
• Show: onboarding / safety / EULA acceptance, lobby, active call with Report + Block visible, drawing overlay if applicable.
• If “Wave to unblur” is on, you may show blurred video in screenshots to match actual behavior.
