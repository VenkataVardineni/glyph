App Store Connect — Demo / Review account (Glyph)
==================================================

1) Deploy the signal server (server/) with environment variables:

   REVIEW_DEMO_USERNAME=apple_review_glyph
   REVIEW_DEMO_PASSWORD=<generate a strong password>
   REVIEW_DEMO_USER_ID=glyph-apple-review

   Optional production:
   ADMIN_TOKEN=<long random secret>
   REDIS_URL=<your redis>   # recommended so reports/blocks/bans survive restarts

2) In App Store Connect → App Review Information, provide:

   Username: (same as REVIEW_DEMO_USERNAME)
   Password: (same as REVIEW_DEMO_PASSWORD)

3) On device, open Glyph → Sign-in → expand “App Review sign-in” → enter those credentials.

4) Ensure EXPO_PUBLIC_SERVER_URL points to the same server instance the reviewer will reach (HTTPS in production).

5) Ban test: after review, rotate REVIEW_DEMO_PASSWORD and ADMIN_TOKEN.
